🔗 Community Mission and Vision

To educate and develop Community members (Including Students and Lecturers) of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka with basic knowledge of Cryptocurrency on Blockchain Education & Development. We have the vision of being the best Campus Blockchain Community in Africa.

🔗 First-ever Blockchain UNN Campus Online Meet up

To further spread the Blockchain education, the first online webinar kicking off the establishment was held on Sunday, 17th October, 2011. Over 32+ students were present and educated at this webinar.

FOCUS

Exploring and Educating Community members on different facets and applications of Blockchain Technology.
